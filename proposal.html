FISH BUSINESS SETUP PROPOSAL

Project Overview

This proposal outlines the setup of a fish storage and processing business equipped with a cold room, deep freezers, and essential tools to ensure proper preservation, hygiene, and efficient sales operations.

Objectives

  • Establish a reliable fish storage system
  • Maintain freshness and reduce spoilage
  • Enable efficient processing and sales
  • Create a scalable and profitable business
